Editorial principles

We are a publication, not an app store.

If you want an open marketplace where anyone can list a skill, there are several. We are the opposite: every listing is reviewed by a human and a model before it goes up.

01

Curation over transaction

One good skill a week beats fifty auto-imported ones. We review, test, and disclose.

02

Funding is visible

Every skill shows who funds it, who maintains it, and where the rebate goes. No dark money.

03

Manual first, automated later

Third-party submission only after the review workflow is real and the trust bar holds.

04

Public-good rebate

30% of every commission funds upstream open-source AI projects. It is on the homepage, not buried.
