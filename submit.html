Submission intake

Submit your skill to the catalog.

Hermesskills accepts a small number of curated submissions per quarter. We review for trust, real-world testing, and editorial fit. Submission is by email — no automated acceptance yet.

Submission criteria

  • SKILL.md frontmatter with name, version, author, license, platforms.
  • MIT or compatible open license.
  • Clear description with load triggers (the "use when..." pattern).
  • At least one reference implementation or worked example.
  • Maintainer willing to respond to issues within 30 days.
  • For clawhub-compatible submissions: a skill.json or equivalent manifest.

How to submit

Email pdbjork@gmail.com with the subject hermosskills submission: <skill-name>. Attach or link: the SKILL.md, the GitHub repo, and a one-paragraph statement of editorial fit.

Review SLA: 14 days. Acceptance: about 1 in 4. We turn down more than we accept because the catalog only grows one at a time, by hand.
