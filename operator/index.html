How the pilot works

Same pattern we use on our own P1 businesses: autonomous prep, human gate on anything external.

  1. Fit call (20 min)

    One income surface. Clear success metric. Go / no-go.

  2. Daily operator work

    Ship or prepare: funnel fixes, drafts, checklists, reports on your stack.

  3. You approve outbounds

    Full preview queue. Yes / no / yes-run. Nothing leaves without you.

  4. Weekly metrics + handoff

    Revenue signals, funnel health, blockers, kill list. You keep the system.

Included vs excluded

Clarity beats hype. Here’s the boundary.

Included

  • Daily async operator work on one primary surface
  • Approval queue for external actions
  • Weekly metrics report
  • End-of-pilot written handoff
  • Optional light secondary surface on Standard+

Not included

  • Unattended email / DM / social blasts
  • Legal, tax, or banking advice
  • Guaranteed revenue or paid ads spend
  • Building a brand-new product from zero idea
  • “Set and forget” full autonomy
