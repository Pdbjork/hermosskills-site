Best fit

A solo founder or tiny team with a live product, Stripe or checkout enabled, and no time to convert useful agent work into shipped revenue tasks.

  • One clear metric for four weeks.
  • Founder can approve work 2–3 hours per week.
  • Distribution, funnel, ops, or trust proof is the bottleneck.

Hard boundary: no unattended email, DMs, social posts, payment changes, deploys, or regulated advice.

What the pilot includes

The buyer pays for shipped operator work plus a reusable approval system, not for a chatbot subscription.

Daily autonomous prep

Landing page edits, lead magnet drafts, funnel checks, backlog cleanup, public proof pages, and small repo/site changes that support revenue.

Approval queue

Every outbound send, public post, deploy, or payment mutation sits in a preview queue with yes, no, and yes-run choices.

Weekly autonomous report

Revenue, followers, subscribers, leads, changed files, commands run, blockers, and the next best action in plain language.

Included

  • One primary business surface.
  • Daily async operator pass on weekdays.
  • Human approval queue and audit trail.
  • Weekly metrics report and end-of-pilot handoff.
  • Light secondary surface on Standard or Intensive.

Excluded

  • Cold outbound without explicit approval.
  • Legal, tax, banking, payroll, KYC, or compliance advice.
  • Paid ad spend or guaranteed revenue.
  • Building a brand-new product from a blank idea.
  • Any “set and forget AI employee” claim.
