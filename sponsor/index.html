Why this exists

Agent teams keep reusing small operational skills: checkout launch checklists, domain checks, safety gates, browser QA, and deployment runbooks. The tools work only if someone maintains the instructions, tests the failure paths, and names the human stop points.

Most marketplaces reward volume. Hermosskills rewards maintained, reviewed, explainable skills.

What a sponsor funds

  • Human review for scope, secrets, reversibility, and human gates.
  • Plain-English notes about what the skill can and cannot do.
  • Disclosure of sponsor relationships in the catalog.

What we will not claim

  • No fake logos, invented customers, or hidden placements.
  • No “safe forever” security stamp.
  • No unattended money movement, public posting, email, or deployment.
  • No legal, tax, banking, or compliance advice.
