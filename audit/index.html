What “Audited v0” means

Scope

The SKILL.md says what it helps with, what it refuses to cover, and who should use it.

Secrets

The skill must keep live keys, tokens, and passwords out of chat, logs, and copied examples.

Human gates

Money movement, outbound messages, public posts, and deploys require explicit human approval.

Reversibility

Risky steps include a stop point, rollback note, or verification command before the user proceeds.

Evidence

The page links to the skill, catalog JSON, and a dated review stamp. No hidden logos or invented customers.

Limits

Audited v0 is not a penetration test, SOC 2 report, legal advice, or malware guarantee.
